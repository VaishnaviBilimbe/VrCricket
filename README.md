## 🌟 Overview

**VR Cricket** brings the thrill of cricket to virtual reality, placing you right in the middle of a packed stadium for the finals! Built in **under one week** during XR Bootcamp, this immersive experience demonstrates the potential of VR technology in sports simulation and gaming.

### 🎯 The Experience
Feel the pressure of the finals as you stand at the crease, virtual bat in hand, with thousands of virtual spectators watching your every move. Will you hit it for six or get bowled out?

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🏟️ **Authentic Stadium Scene**
- **Cricket Pitch**: Professionally designed pitch with accurate dimensions
- **Stadium Atmosphere**: Complete with boundary ropes, spectator stands, and crowd
- **Immersive Audio**: Stadium ambiance and crowd reactions

### ⚾ **Realistic Batting Mechanics**
- **Physics-Driven Collisions**: Advanced ball-bat interaction system
- **Controller Mapping**: VR controller movements directly translate to bat swings
- **Dynamic Ball Physics**: Realistic ball trajectories with spin and loft mechanics
- **Swing Speed & Angle Detection**: Precise tracking of your batting technique

### 📊 **Dynamic Scoring System**
- **Auto-Detection**: Intelligent system recognizes 4s, 6s, and wicket dismissals
- **Real-Time Updates**: Live scoreboard that updates instantly with your performance
- **Match Statistics**: Track your batting performance throughout the session

## 🎮 Getting Started
### Prerequisites
- Unity 2022.3 or later
- VR headset (Meta Quest 2+)
- VR development environment setup
- Adequate play space (2m x 2m minimum recommended)

### Installation
```bash
# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/VijayKagupati/VR-Cricket.git

# Open in Unity
# File → Open Project → Select VR-Cricket folder

# Build to your headset
```

### Quick Start Guide
1. Put on your VR headset and ensure controllers are tracked
2. Launch VR Cricket from Unity or built executable
3. Grab the virtual cricket bat with your dominant hand
4. Position yourself at the crease
5. Watch the ball and swing naturally - just like real cricket!
6. Aim for the boundaries and watch your score climb!
